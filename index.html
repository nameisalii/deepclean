Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

How long does a standard cleaning take?
A typical 3-bedroom house takes 3–4 hours with a 2-person team. Larger homes or deep cleans may take longer.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No—all professional-grade, eco-friendly supplies and equipment are included in the price.
Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?
Yes. Every team member is fully insured, bonded, and has passed a thorough background check.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations with at least 24 hours notice are free. Same-day cancellations incur a $50 fee.
Do you offer recurring discounts?
Yes! Weekly clients save 15%, bi-weekly save 10%, and monthly save 5%.
