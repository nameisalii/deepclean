About DeepClean

Trusted by Los Angeles families for over 6 years

Founded with Passion, Built on Trust

DeepClean was founded 6 years ago by a dedicated couple who saw a need for reliable, high-quality cleaning services in Los Angeles. What started as a small family business has grown into the most trusted cleaning company serving the entire LA area.

Today, we proudly serve over 1,000 satisfied customers and maintain 100 weekly clients who trust us to keep their homes and offices spotless. Our professional team is fully trained, insured, and committed to excellence in every clean.
